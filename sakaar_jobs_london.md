## TIPS FOR LANDING THESE ROLES

1. **Build a portfolio site** - Show don't tell
2. **Network at BFI events** - Film London runs regular meetups
3. **Start as a runner** - The classic entry point for production
4. **Get on Trainee Finder** - ScreenSkills program places trainees on major productions
5. **Join BECTU** - Union for media workers, access to job board
6. **Follow company LinkedIn pages** - Jobs often posted there first
7. **Attend festivals** - Sheffield Doc/Fest, London Film Festival have industry days
